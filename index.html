C-SPECT, Inc.

Tim Clement incorporated C-Spect, Inc. in 1982. We are an established project management and visual inspection company. C-SADDLE™, LLC

C-SADDLE™ is the next generation protection between the pipe and pipe support that stops dangerous crevice corrosion. Our C-SADDLE™ uses a superior wear resistant structural NSM nylon sliding pad that has solid lubricant imbedded in the material that is over 300 times more abrasion resistant than Teflon with a similar coefficient of friction. C-SADDLE™ is the best sliding pipe shoe on the market!
