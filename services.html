C-Spect Services

 

How can we help?

We are committed to supplying our customers with the most competent, professional and ethical services available. In an industry where change and evolution is a daily occurrence, we continuously strive to remain a leader in our field.

Tim Clement incorporated C-Spect, Inc. in 1982. We are an established project management and visual inspection company. We offer a wide range of expertise and experience in the construction of oilfield production equipment, pipelines and onshore/offshore installations.

During the past 35 years, C-Spect, Inc. has provided management and inspection services for key projects for many major and independent oil companies. These projects have included domestic and foreign on site surveillance and supervision services. Some of the locations include Trinidad, Tunisia, Ecuador, Russia, Gabon and the US Gulf of Mexico.

C-Spect, Inc. has the ability to provide management, supervision and inspection teams to our customers to fit the requirements to achieve quality projects in a professional and timely manner.

As a third party project management and inspection company, C-Spect, Inc. offers a very high level of quality service. Our personnel are carefully selected and highly proficient in their area of expertise. Our goal is to provide services in which each professional and their skills are matched to meet the individual project needs of our clients.

We, at C-Spect, Inc. are pleased to have this opportunity to submit for your review personnel and resumes for your upcoming projects. Our goal is to provide an excellent program, which fits our client’s needs and is efficient and competitive. You can be assured of the best possible service and attention on this or any future projects.
